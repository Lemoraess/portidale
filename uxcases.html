Case Study: Insurance company for lawyers

Products: Simplified coverage (higher sales) and broader coverage.
Main issues identified:

  • Navbar - with links that led to the same page, lack of company description (About page), only one unprominent contact link, and no other access points on the page (only via WhatsApp link).
  • Sad images - The company attempts to connect with the user's pain by highlighting potential problems, but fails to present the product solution with the same prominence.
  • Layout - Bland background, long texts with small, black font. Lack of visual appeal in the content.
  • Section Distribution - Only a small section for other products and little emphasis on the main product - the company focuses on the problems the user may experience.

Changes and Proposals:

The primary objective was to create a robust and visually appealing website that conveys a sense of security and quality.

  • Stronger, more impactful header.
  • Defined and purposeful color palette - with blue and gold | colors usually associated with excellence and stability.
  • Images of confident, strong lawyers â€” the firm's mission and goal for their clients.
  • Emphasis on simplified hiring, but also on other coverage options.
  • Inclusion of "About" and "Contact" pages for greater access to major information (maintaining the WhatsApp link upon client request) - giving the clients more confidence to close the deal.
  • Inclusion of the firm's social media channels and information in the footer.
  • Make both themes, lighter and dark, to attend to clients' preferences.
