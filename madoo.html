MADOO UX Case

This case was proposed during a UX Designer selection process and fully designed by Lenise Santana.

Designed to optimize the work of specialists, MADOO is a program that uses Artificial Intelligence to organize, store, prioritize, and analyze documents.

The name comes from "Managing Documents and Others" and aims to personalize the program, enabling a closer relationship with the user through direct interaction with the AI ​​via a chat box.

MADOO was designed for mature users who require practicality and security through effective and simple solutions. It was designed for desktop and mobile use, and offers two color themes—light and dark—to accommodate visual comfort and user preference.
