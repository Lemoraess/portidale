Lenise is a UX Designer specializing in Healthcare UX, with a decade of frontline experience in mental health services in São Paulo — including 7 years working with homeless populations and substance use disorders in Cracolândia, the largest open drug scene in Latin America. That background shaped a design practice built on deep user research, systems thinking, and equity-centered solutions. Now she applies that lens to digital health products — combining clinical insight with UX methodology and frontend skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.
